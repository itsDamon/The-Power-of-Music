Description
This text can be defined as a poetry or a music track that expresses feelings of defeat, resignation and
bitterness. In this song I’m talking about a difficult life and a society in which I’m not feeling understood or
appreciated. The relationship with a girl, who is mentioned, is described as complex and conflicting, with
feelings of love and hate. I also write about how I'm engaged in an inner struggle with myself and the
demons I carry within. Overall my text serves to convey the sense of loneliness and it is an escape from
reality that I have had over the years and that is probably still present in my head today.
Thematic References
Social distress in adolescents can have many causes, including family problems, difficulties at school,
bullying, social isolation, substance abuse, depression and anxiety. These problems can lead to feelings of
alienation, despair and frustration. Adolescents may feel powerless and unable to find a solution to their
problems.
To escape from reality, some adolescents use harmful methods of adaptation, such as drugs, alcoholism or
gambling addiction. Others do it through social isolation, disengagement from daily activities or
oversleeping.
However, there are positive ways to deal with social discomfort. For example, by seeking support from
friends, family or professionals, such as psychologists or social workers, or through activities such as sport,
art or meditation.
