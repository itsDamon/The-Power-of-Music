copycat speaks of the change in both character and physical nature of one person in order to please another, he denounces the error and mistake of this action. one should never "create a version of oneself" in order to please another person, I am of the opinion that if you have to please someone, you have to please them because it is you, not the idea they create of you. to be loved knowing that if you knew the truest part of yourself, the other person would leave is not to love, it's just an ideal and also quite sad. you should live and be loved knowing that you are unique, not because you are someone else.
