Description
“Bones” is a song written for an American series “The boys”, right after the singer lost his sister-in-law to cancer. In the lyrics you see the singer reflecting in front of a mirror of his past, trying to not lose himself and moving forward. The author wants to encourage the people in bad situations to stay strong and overcome the adversities they encounter, despite the difficulties.
Thematic References
Difficulties are present everywhere, whether one likes it or not. The biggest challenge in life is not the problem itself, but how you react to it. The way you react to a challenge can make you stronger or weaker, more or less successful. One may want to achieve results without effort, but those difficulties are essential in forming an individual, and since it is certain that they will arise again in life, it is better not to run away and get used to them so that you can handle those that you encounter again in the future. In fact you can’t get through life without making mistakes or without learning.
