Description
In Gazo's song 'DIE', the rapper explores the idea of striving and doing everything possible to achieve his goals until death. The song outlines the hard work he puts in and the efforts he makes to become successful in whatever he does. He states that even when it gets difficult or people doubt him, he will continue to push forward. Despite everything that happens to him and regardless of the outcome, he will fight for his dreams until the end. The song reaffirms the idea that life is short, so he wants to do everything he can to make the most of what he has and keep going. Gazo takes it rather badly that people only appear in his life after he has found success. Moreover, his background makes him resent people who pretend to know the way when he has actually lived it.
