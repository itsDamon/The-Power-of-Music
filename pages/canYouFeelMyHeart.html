Description
the song discusses the antifraction between not wanting to be alone and the desire to have someone by one's side to stand by one's side and the internal struggle between one's demons.
Rimando tematica
Many people suffer in tranquillity, some people create problems when everything goes well, because they have never been well, and when things go as they should, they feel uncomfortable, this happens when you carry chaos inside you. the desire to want to be recognised as someone who exists and someone's affection or love is a very normal feeling but for some people this clashes with the unhealthy feeling of feeling isolated, as if they do not exist or are excluded from the world these inner demons have to be faced, running away from oneself will never make them feel free but above all, they will never feel accepted and will never appreciate themselves as much as they should.
