Description
This song is inspired by a WWII concentration camp survivor. The author shows the horrible side of war and its stupidity in the lyrics. It's about a unexperienced soldier marching in war and meets another one exactly like himself, a guy with no experience in war, and is risking his life for futile war. And since the uniform colour is different the fight is inevitable.

The Nazists have the elimination of the local population and reinforcement of the superior Aryan race according to nazisms as their objective.
