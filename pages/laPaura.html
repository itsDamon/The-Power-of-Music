Description
we can divide the song into 2 parts, the first where the artist talks about his fear and his anxiety in a fairly well-established state of popularity. The second part, on the other hand, attacks society, especially the Italian one, where the most important topic that is talked about inside is gossip. In this part we find the description of the social anxiety that many people feel about not being up to other people.
