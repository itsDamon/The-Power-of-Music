Description
The song can be defined as an anthem of protest towards the school system, which was expressed through the complaints of children against a rigid education, capable of brainwashing the pupils. "We don’t need no education / We don’t need no thought control". The song can also be protest against the homogenisation of people in the society, without a difference from the people and this is the reason of the wall , it divide the people
