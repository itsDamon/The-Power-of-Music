Description
The lyrics of Logic's song "1-800-273-8255" are an anthem for suicide prevention and emotional support for those struggling with depression and anxiety. The title of the song is the phone number for the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline in the United States, which offers free and confidential support to anyone in need. The protagonist reflects on their problems and the reasons why they feel so bad, but ultimately decides to call the suicide prevention hotline number. The song ends with the voice of a suicide prevention hotline operator talking to the protagonist and offering emotional support. The lyrics of the song are an invitation to not be ashamed to ask for help when struggling with depression and anxiety, and to seek support from friends, family, and mental health professionals. The song aims to convey the message that life can be difficult and painful at times, but there are resources available to help people overcome difficulties and live a happy and fulfilling life.
